Activities - Student Science Club

Discover What We Do!

  • 1. Weekly Science Experiments

    Every week, we dive into exciting hands-on experiments, from building volcanic eruptions with baking soda to exploring the physics of motion with simple machines.

  • 2. Interactive Workshops

    Join expert-led sessions on topics like robotics, chemistry magic tricks, astronomy, and more. Learn from professionals and gain unique insights into the scientific world!

  • 3. Field Trips

    Experience science outside the classroom! We organize visits to research labs, museums, planetariums, and nature reserves to see science in action.

  • 4. Science Fairs and Competitions

    Unleash your creativity by participating in inter-school and regional science fairs. Show off your innovative projects and learn from others!

  • 5. Fun Science Challenges

    Compete in exciting challenges like bridge-building contests, science quizzes, and more. Test your skills and have fun with your peers!
